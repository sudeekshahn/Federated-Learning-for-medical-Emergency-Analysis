💡 KEY INSIGHTS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. ALGORITHM SELECTION MATTERS
   Different algorithms have dramatically different emergency response times
   - FCFS: 35% emergency compliance
   - Priority-Based: 98% emergency compliance
   - 63% improvement = potential lives saved

2. TRADE-OFFS ARE NECESSARY
   Better emergency response → Lower fairness to regular patients
   But this is acceptable in healthcare (life > fairness)

3. SLA COMPLIANCE IS CRITICAL
   Medical emergencies have strict time requirements
   Priority-Based is the only algorithm that reliably meets them

4. COST-BENEFIT ANALYSIS
   Priority-Based costs slightly more but provides essential service
   In healthcare, the cost is justified by improved outcomes

5. SYSTEM DESIGN MATTERS
   Good scheduling makes the difference between acceptable and excellent systems
   Architecture decisions impact patient outcomes
